Do You Know?

Wisdom of yoga, Ayurvedic herbs & the science behind every pose

01
🌅

Yoga is 5,000+ Years Old

The word "yoga" first appears in the Rigveda (~1500 BCE). From ancient Indian forests to modern studios worldwide — it is one of humanity's oldest living sciences of wellbeing.

02
🧠

Meditation Physically Rewires Your Brain

Harvard neuroscientists found that after 8 weeks of daily mindfulness, the amygdala (fear centre) shrinks, and the prefrontal cortex (reasoning) measurably thickens. Your brain literally grows in wisdom.

03
💪

One Flow Works All Major Muscles

Surya Namaskar (Sun Salutation) activates all 12 major muscle groups in one continuous sequence. Doing 12 rounds burns approximately 156 calories — the equivalent of 20 minutes of running.

04
🩺

Yoga Cuts Cortisol by 30%

Peer-reviewed studies show that 12 weeks of regular yoga practice reduces cortisol (the primary stress hormone) by up to 30%, lowering blood pressure and improving immune function simultaneously.

05
🫁

Your Breath Controls Your Nervous System

Slow, deep breathing (5–6 breaths/min) activates the parasympathetic nervous system within 90 seconds. This is faster than any supplement — and it's built into you. Pranayama is the original biohack.

06
🌍

500 Million People Meditate Daily

An estimated 500 million people worldwide practice some form of meditation. It transcends religion, culture, and language — from Tibetan monks to corporate boardrooms to Olympic training camps.

07
🌙

Yoga Transforms Sleep Quality

Regular practitioners fall asleep 15 minutes faster on average, experience 40% longer deep-sleep phases, and wake 30% less during the night. Even a 10-minute Yoga Nidra session equals 1 hour of deep sleep.

08
🔬

Yoga Raises Your Brain's GABA

Boston University MRI studies found yoga increases GABA levels by 27% after a single session. GABA is the same calming neurotransmitter targeted by anti-anxiety medications — yoga achieves it naturally.

Ayurveda — yoga's sister science — has documented these herbs over 5,000 years. They work synergistically with yoga practice to amplify mental clarity, physical strength, and emotional resilience.

🌿

Ashwagandha

Withania somnifera · Indian Ginseng
Adaptogen

The king of Ayurvedic herbs. Ashwagandha means "smell of horse" — it gives you the strength and vitality of a horse. Used for 3,000 years to build resilience to physical and mental stress.

🧠 MindReduces cortisol & anxiety, improves sleep, sharpens memory and focus
💪 BodyIncreases muscle strength & endurance, reduces inflammation, boosts testosterone
How to use: ½ tsp powder in warm milk or water before bed. Or 300–500mg capsule with meals.
🌱

Tulsi (Holy Basil)

Ocimum tenuiflorum · Queen of Herbs
Sacred

Called "The Queen of Herbs" in Ayurveda, Tulsi is sacred in every Hindu household. This powerful adaptogen has been used for millennia to purify the body, mind, and environment.

🧠 MindElevates mood, combats mental fatigue, reduces anxiety and emotional stress
💪 BodyAntibacterial, antifungal, supports respiratory & immune health, lowers blood sugar
How to use: Brew 5–7 fresh leaves in hot water for 5 minutes. Drink daily as morning tea. Or Tulsi drops (10 drops in water).
🧠

Brahmi

Bacopa monnieri · Water Hyssop
Nootropic

Named after Brahma — the Hindu god of creation — Brahmi is yoga's favourite brain herb. Ancient scholars consumed it for memory enhancement and accelerated learning. Modern neuroscience confirms it works.

🧠 MindEnhances memory formation, reduces anxiety, improves concentration and cognitive speed
💪 BodyReduces oxidative stress, supports nervous system, anti-inflammatory for brain tissue
How to use: 2–3 drops Brahmi oil massaged into scalp nightly. Or 300 mg Bacopa extract capsule with breakfast. Takes 4–6 weeks to see peak effects.
💛

Turmeric

Curcuma longa · Golden Spice
Anti-inflammatory

Turmeric's active compound curcumin is one of the most studied natural substances. It crosses the blood-brain barrier, reduces neuroinflammation, and genuinely rivals antidepressants in clinical trials.

🧠 MindBoosts serotonin & dopamine, reduces depression, protects against cognitive decline
💪 BodyPowerful anti-inflammatory, joint protection, liver detox, antioxidant at cellular level
How to use: Golden milk: 1 tsp turmeric + pinch of black pepper in warm milk. The pepper increases curcumin absorption by 2000%. Daily with meals.
🌸

Shatavari

Asparagus racemosus · She Who Has 100 Husbands
Rejuvenating

Shatavari is Ayurveda's premier tonic for vitality, hormonal harmony, and deep nourishment. While especially revered for women, it is equally beneficial for all — promoting calm, strength and longevity.

🧠 MindReduces anxiety & emotional instability, promotes calm, stabilises mood
💪 BodyHormonal balance, immune strength, digestive health, anti-aging, builds Ojas (vital essence)
How to use: 1 tsp Shatavari powder in warm milk or water, twice daily. Best taken with honey or ghee to enhance absorption.
🫐

Triphala

Three Sacred Fruits: Amalaki · Bibhitaki · Haritaki
Detox

Triphala is Ayurveda's most revered formula — three fruits combined for a synergistic effect no single herb achieves alone. Used for 2,000+ years as the complete internal cleanser and rejuvenator.

🧠 MindClears mental fog through gut-brain axis, reduces stress, supports sharp cognitive function
💪 BodyDetoxifies all three doshas, improves digestion & elimination, rich in Vitamin C, antioxidant
How to use: ½ tsp Triphala powder in warm water at bedtime. Or 500mg tablet. Start with smaller dose and increase gradually to avoid digestive adjustment.
🌿

Moringa

Moringa oleifera · The Miracle Tree
Superfood

Moringa contains 92 nutrients, 46 antioxidants, and all 9 essential amino acids. Gram for gram: 7x the Vitamin C of oranges, 4x the calcium of milk, 3x the iron of spinach. A complete nutritional powerhouse.

🧠 MindCombats brain fog, improves mental clarity and memory, natural mood elevator
💪 BodySustained clean energy, reduces inflammation, regulates blood sugar, strengthens bones
How to use: 1 tsp Moringa leaf powder in morning smoothie, juice, or water. Or add to soups and dals. Start with ½ tsp if new to it.
🍃

Giloy (Guduchi)

Tinospora cordifolia · Heart-Leaved Moonseed
Immunity

Called "Amrita" (divine nectar of immortality) in Sanskrit, Giloy is Ayurveda's premier immune tonic. It was documented during the 2020 pandemic for its remarkable immune-modulating properties.

🧠 MindReduces stress and anxiety, promotes mental clarity, known as "medhya rasayana" (mind tonic)
💪 BodyPowerful immunity booster, anti-inflammatory, liver protection, detoxifies blood, reduces fever
How to use: 2–4 tsp Giloy juice on empty stomach each morning. Or Giloy kadha (decoction). Or 500mg capsule twice daily with water.
